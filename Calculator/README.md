# 🧮 Smart Calculator

A modern, feature-rich calculator built with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.  
Designed with a **Glassmorphism UI**, responsive layout, and advanced functionality suitable for both basic and scientific calculations.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Modern Glassmorphism UI**
- ✅ **Dark/Light Mode** (saved in localStorage)
- ✅ **Live Clock**
- ✅ **Basic Arithmetic Operations** (+, −, ×, ÷)
- ✅ **Scientific Functions** (√, x², 1/x, sin, cos, tan, log, ln, π, e)
- ✅ **Calculation History** (persistent across sessions)
- ✅ **Keyboard Support** (numbers, operators, Enter, Backspace, Escape)
- ✅ **Copy Result to Clipboard**
- ✅ **Backspace & Clear**
- ✅ **Percentage & ± Toggle**
- ✅ **Memory Buttons** (MC, MR, M+, M-, MS)
- ✅ **Responsive Design** (mobile-friendly)
- ✅ **Smooth Animations**
- ✅ **Error Handling**
- ✅ **Auto Font Resize** for long results
